Plan 2 - Space Resident

Our Space Resident package offers a unique opportunity for individuals to conduct scientific experiments and research while living and working aboard the International Space Station (ISS) for a week. As a Space Resident, you'll receive intensive training on how to operate complex equipment and conduct research in a microgravity environment. You'll also learn about the various ongoing experiments and projects being conducted on the ISS and contribute to the scientific community with your own research.

Destination

To the International Space Station!

Travel Duration

Cost

During your stay on the ISS, you'll be living and working in an environment that requires careful attention to details and protocols to ensure the safety of all crew members. As a Space Resident, you'll be an integral part of the ongoing mission of the ISS to advance scientific understanding and exploration of space.
