The Shuttle's Trajectory

The Training You Will Receive

Over the course of 2 years you will learn about many things besides science, also about medical procedures. To feel what you will be doing in space you will be practicing on life-sized models also known as “mock-ups”. The Space Vehicle Mock-up Facility (SVMF) is where you will practice. To get a feel for zero gravity you will board a place known as the Vomit Comet which will provide you with 20-25 seconds of zero gravity. You will practice extravehicular activities, or space walks, and to do this you use the Neutral Buoyancy Laboratory (NBL), a huge "swimming pool." You will be practicing on full-sized models of space vehicles.
